Just copy/paste one of the .snippet files and edit it in notepad/wordpad or your favorite XML editor!

You can use $variables$ in your snippets, that let you replace the same text in multiple place simultaniously.
Just be sure that you define these in the appropriate "Literal" section in the XML file.

If you want your snippets to ship with RepDev, email them to support@repdev.org
Just be sure that you are OK with them being licensed under the GPL in your name.
